Drawdio

Drawdio is a mobile drawing application where users build visual & sonic environments by selecting a background, drawing & placing stickers. Each background, sticker, and brush stroke create audio that work together to build a layered musical composition. Drawdio aims to improve the mood of college students through self expression, playful engagement, and creative empowerment.


Lead Game Developer / Associate UI Developer

• A 2D digital illustration app that allows users to create visual and auditory environments by choosing backgrounds, drawing, and placing stickers.
• Collaborated with a team of 5 to focus on the project's inclusion of art and interactive electronic music therapies.
• Developed core features including line drawing, color adjustments, undo/clear options, stickers, and interactive music.
• Designed UI/UX elements like the navigation bar, background selectors, and color tabs, and conducted user testing to improve the app.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a playable/virtual game lets two players take turns marking Xs and Os on a 3x3 grid, aiming to be the first to align their three symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game ends when one player wins or all cells are filled, resulting in a draw.


Game Developer

• Designed and implemented a playable Tic-Tac-Toe game for two users, using sprites to create the grid and X/O objects.
• Developed functionality for placing pieces based on mouse clicks, ensuring placement in valid locations and preventing overwrites.
• Programmed turn-based mechanics to alternate between players and incorporated win/stalemate detection with result presentation.
• Created and presented as a demonstration of Unity's 2D capabilities, as part of the Learning Assistant curriculum.

Baking In The Woods

Within a peaceful forest setting near the mountains, players venture into a cozy wooden cabin to experience the quiet, meditative atmosphere of baking. The goal of the game is to explore the cabin and its surroundings to locate the ingredients needed to bake a fresh loaf of bread. As you search through cupboards, counters, and the natural environment outside, Baking In The Woods invites you to unwind, take in the scenery, and enjoy the calming process of creating something warm and comforting.


Game Developer

• Designed and implemented a first-person playable prototype exploring the theme “My Life Then, Now, and Future.”
• Enabled camera movement using the mouse and player movement with W/A/S/D or arrow keys.
• Incorporated interactive elements to reflect personal growth, preferences, and coping with change.
• Polished the prototype to enhance user experience and thematic expression.
