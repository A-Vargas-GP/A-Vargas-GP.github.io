This case study explores the "Adventure Aquarium Scavenger Hunt" app, designed to engage visitors in a pirate-themed scavenger hunt with prize opportunities. It includes personas, mock ethnographic research, and usability testing with System Usability Scales and think-aloud methods. This analysis presents how the UX effectively meets its intended goals, while highlighting key strengths and areas for improvement.


UI/UX Researcher

• Developed a user persona for the "Adventure Aquarium Scavenger Hunt" app, identifying potential issues and proposing solutions to enhance user engagement and address specific challenges.
• Created and administered a System Usability Scale (SUS) form and a think-aloud testing session, analyzing the app’s usability and effectiveness in meeting user needs.
• Designed the presentation to align with the app's pirate-themed aesthetic, ensuring a cohesive and engaging report.
